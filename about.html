Welcome to Darussalam Banath

Darussalam Banath is a dedicated Islamic educational institution that blends academic excellence with Islamic teachings. We provide a nurturing environment where students grow intellectually and spiritually, ensuring they are well-prepared for both worldly and religious success.

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to create a generation of knowledgeable and ethical individuals who excel in both faith and profession. We strive to provide high-quality education, instill moral values, and cultivate leadership qualities in our students.

What We Offer

- Islamic Children's Primary School

A strong foundation in both academic and Islamic education, focusing on character building and discipline.

- Islamic Higher Secondary Madrasa

Advanced religious and academic studies to prepare students for higher education and lifelong learning.

- Islamic Women's College

Our women's college empowers female students with knowledge, confidence, and leadership skills while maintaining a strong Islamic foundation. We provide higher education opportunities that enable them to contribute to their families, communities, and professions with integrity and excellence.
